# Sales Analysis Using Power BI

## Project Overview

This project focuses on analyzing sales performance using Microsoft Power BI to uncover business insights related to revenue, profitability, customer behavior, regional performance, and operational efficiency. The dashboard transforms raw sales data into meaningful visualizations that support data-driven decision-making.

The project demonstrates practical applications of data modeling, DAX calculations, KPI reporting, and interactive dashboard development.

---

## Business Objective

The primary objective of this project is to evaluate sales performance and identify opportunities for business growth through data analysis.

Key objectives include:

* Monitoring overall sales and profit performance.
* Identifying top-performing product categories.
* Analyzing regional sales trends.
* Evaluating customer purchasing behavior.
* Measuring the impact of discounts on profitability.
* Supporting strategic business decisions through visual analytics.

---

## Dataset Overview

The dataset contains transactional sales data, including:

* Order ID
* Customer Information
* Product Categories
* Sales Revenue
* Profit
* Discount
* Shipping Mode
* Regional Data

The data was cleaned, transformed, and modeled before visualization and analysis.

## Key Findings

* Certain product categories contribute significantly to overall revenue.
* A small group of customers generates a large portion of total sales.
* Regional performance varies considerably across markets.
* Discounts influence profitability differently depending on product categories.
* Shipping methods impact operational efficiency and business performance.

## Business Insights

* Focus on high-performing product categories to maximize revenue.
* Strengthen customer retention strategies for top customers.
* Optimize discount policies to protect profit margins.
