# Amazon Sales Analytics Dashboard

## Project Overview

This project focuses on analyzing Amazon product data using Microsoft Power BI to evaluate product performance, customer engagement, pricing strategies, discounts, and review trends. The dashboard transforms raw product and customer feedback data into actionable business insights that support product optimization and strategic decision-making.

The project demonstrates practical applications of data modeling, KPI reporting, interactive dashboards, and business intelligence analytics.

---

## Business Objective

The primary objective of this project is to analyze Amazon product performance and identify opportunities to improve customer engagement and business outcomes.

Key objectives include:

* Evaluate product performance across categories.
* Analyze customer ratings and reviews.
* Assess pricing and discount strategies.
* Measure customer engagement levels.
* Identify top-performing and underperforming products.
* Generate actionable business recommendations.

---

## Dataset Overview

The dataset contains Amazon product and customer review information, including:

* Product ID
* Product Name
* Category
* Actual Price
* Discounted Price
* Discount Percentage
* Rating
* Rating Count
* Product Description
* Customer Reviews
* Customer Information

The data was cleaned, transformed, and prepared for reporting using Power BI.

The dashboard tracks the following business metrics:

## Key Findings

* Highly rated products attract stronger customer engagement.
* A small number of categories contribute a significant portion of business value.
* Larger discounts do not always lead to higher ratings.
* Product performance varies significantly across categories.
* Customer reviews play a critical role in product visibility and engagement.

---

## Business Insights

* Product quality has a stronger impact on ratings than discount levels.
* Customer engagement increases with positive reviews and ratings.
* Pricing strategies should balance competitiveness and profitability.
* Certain categories offer higher business potential and customer interest.

---

## Recommendations

1. Focus marketing efforts on top-performing product categories.
2. Improve product quality and customer experience for low-rated products.
3. Optimize discount strategies based on performance data.
4. Encourage customer reviews and feedback to improve engagement.
5. Use customer sentiment analysis to support product development decisions.
